Most Asked Questions

  • Does Netflix offer a free trial?

    Netflix no longer offers free trials. However, you can sign up and explore different plans that suit your needs.

  • Can I share my Netflix account?

    Yes, Netflix allows you to create multiple profiles on one account. However, account sharing outside your household may be limited.

  • Does Netflix have parental controls?

    Netflix offers parental controls to restrict content by age and set up kid-friendly profiles.

  • What internet speed do I need for Netflix?

    Netflix recommends at least 3 Mbps for SD quality, 5 Mbps for HD, and 25 Mbps for Ultra HD streaming.

  • Can I download shows to watch offline?

    Yes, you can download content on the Netflix app for offline viewing on iOS, Android, or Windows 10 devices.
